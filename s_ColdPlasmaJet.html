ARTIST'S CONCEPTION

Long jets - without any inert gas

We have built a cold plasma jet device that produces 20 mm long plasma jets in the air, using only electrodes: no helium or other inert gas required!

Our portable version has a main unit that can be clipped to the waist. An armored, flexible cable connects to the hand held unit giving ample freedom of movement. Ideal for clinics and hospitals that use CAPPjets for cosmetic treatment and disinfection.

Our array version produces multiple jets and can be connected to a scanning arm to cover large areas. Ideal for disinfecting or cleaning / treating large surfaces.
