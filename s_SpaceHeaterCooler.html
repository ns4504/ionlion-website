ARTIST'S CONCEPTION

Leave it to the ions

We have developed air heating and cooling techniques in our laboratories that have the potential to deliver unique space heating and cooling solutions to diverse markets.

Our systems deliver hot and cool air flows directly out of the electrodes - no refrigerant, compressor, resistive heating element or heat exchanger required. And, of course, no moving parts. You can go continuously from hot to cool (or the other way) simply by turning one knob; the response to a change in setting is instantaneous.

We have embarked on a mission of scaling our techniques to provide simple, lightweight, efficient, low-cost, low-maintenance, eco-friendly heating/cooling products: hot boxes, coolers, incubators, laboratory equipment, refrigerators etc.

Our long term vision is to deliver temperature control systems for cars, homes and buildings, replacing conventional hvac systems that are bulky, inefficient, high-cost, high-maintenance and ecologically damaging.
